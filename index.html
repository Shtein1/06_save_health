Valentine's Day is an annual holiday celebrated on February 14.

Why did Valentine's Day appear?

Saint Valentine – A priest who secretly married couples.

Lupercalia – An ancient Roman fertility festival.

Medieval Love Beliefs – February was seen as the season of romance.

What can I give for Valentine's Day?

  • Personalized Gift – A customized present like an engraved necklace, photo album, or a personalized mug;
  • Romantic Dinner – A candlelit dinner at home or a fancy restaurant;
  • Love Letter or Handmade Card – A heartfelt love letter or a DIY Valentine’s card;
  • Experience Gift – A special experience like a spa day, a cooking class, or concert tickets;
  • Chocolate and Flowers – Classic but always a sweet and romantic choice
